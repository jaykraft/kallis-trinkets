What started as a kitchen-table hobby has grown into something truly special. Kalli's Trinkets & More was born from a deep love of crafting and a desire to bring heartfelt, personalized joy to people's lives.

We're a small family business, and that means everything to us. Every tumbler we engrave and every figurine we print carries a piece of our heart. We believe the best gifts are the ones that tell a story — yours.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, honoring someone special, or just showing someone you care, we're here to help you create something they'll treasure forever.
